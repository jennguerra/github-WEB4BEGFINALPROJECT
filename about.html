Therapeutic Healing and Intuitive Readings by Jenn Guerra

My healing work is a combination of energy work, positive affirmations, reiki and coaching. My sessions are rooted out of love and positive expectation that healing is possible. I truly believe that love is the greatest healer.

The healing realm has always fascinated me. At a young age I was meditating and devouring books on spirituality and self help. After traveling the world I settled in San Francisco to attend massage and meditation school. I established my healing arts business in 2005 in SF. I have been blessed to work with so many people from all walks of life. I am appreciative of all I have learned so far and embrace my ever evolving growth in this world.

I look forward to supporting you along your healing journey. Please contact me anytime!
